Urban Daybreak is a trendy artisan cafe located in the heart of Georgetown, Penang. Founded by a group of friends whom believes in good soul cooking, Urban Daybreak aims to tantalise your tastebuds with authentic Australian breakfast and brunch with a twist. We aim to create delicious and natural food that leaves you craving for more. Step into our little cafe we call home, and come experience a tasty and unique adventure!
